bootcamps

Bootcamps are 4-day workshops open to the entire emlyon community. Students, alumni, staffs, incubated, executives, etc. are welcome!
Through an immersion in the makers' lab technology-based, participants learn makers' skills that will make the difference on their curriculum.
A bootcamp strengthen ties between collaborators while building a product emblematic of a tech that is part of a sustainable approach.
